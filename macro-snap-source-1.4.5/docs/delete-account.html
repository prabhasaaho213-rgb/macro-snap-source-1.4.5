Delete Your Account

Last updated: July 24, 2026

You have the right to delete your MacroSnap account and associated data at any time. Below are the methods available to request account deletion.

What gets deleted:
  • Your phone number and authentication data
  • Your meal history and nutrition logs
  • Your streak data and preferences
  • Your subscription status (if any)
What is retained (if applicable):
  • Anonymous crash reports (no personal identifiers)
  • Transaction records for tax/accounting purposes (90 days)

Option 1: Delete In-App

  1. Open the MacroSnap app
  2. Go to Settings (gear icon in the top-right)
  3. Scroll down and tap "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm the deletion when prompted
  5. Your account and all data will be permanently removed

Option 2: Request via Email

Send an email from the email address associated with your account:

To: macrosnap7@gmail.com
Subject: Account Deletion Request
Body: Please include your registered phone number and the reason for deletion (optional).

Send Deletion Request Email

Processing Time

Account deletion requests are processed within 7 business days. You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.

Data Retention Period

After requesting deletion:

  • Account data is deleted immediately upon processing
  • Cloud backups are purged within 30 days
  • Transaction records are retained for 90 days for legal/accounting purposes

If you have any questions about account deletion or data handling, contact us at macrosnap7@gmail.com.
