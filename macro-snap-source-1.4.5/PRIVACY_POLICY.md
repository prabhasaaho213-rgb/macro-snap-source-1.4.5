# Privacy Policy for MacroSnap

**Last updated: July 2, 2026**

## Data Collection
MacroSnap does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

## Camera & Photos
The app uses your camera and photo gallery only to analyze food images. Photos are sent to a server for AI analysis and are **not stored or shared** beyond processing the nutrition results.

## Server Communication
When you scan food, the image is sent to a privately hosted server for AI analysis using Google Gemini API. No images or personal data are retained.

## Third-Party Services
MacroSnap uses:
- **Google Gemini API** – for food recognition and nutrition analysis
- **UPI apps** (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm) – for subscription payments
