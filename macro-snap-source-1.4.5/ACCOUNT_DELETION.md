# Account Deletion Request — MacroSnap

## How to Delete Your Account and Data

### Option 1: Email Request (Recommended)
Send an email to **macrosnap7@gmail.com** with the subject **"Account Deletion Request"** and include the phone number or email address you used to sign up.

We will process your request within **7 business days**.

A self-service account deletion option will be available in a future update.
